Profil

I am a newly graduated it-product developer, driven by working with and creating good experiences for end-users. My background is an M.Sc. in IT-Product Development, from Aarhus University under Department of Computer Science, which is a mix of computer science, HCI, UX, and architectural courses. With this broad knowledge I can be a part of every step in a development process, from generating ideas to implementing and evaluating systems with and without users.

My passion for technology is rooted in its’ endless possibilities. Therefore, the projects I like explore new potentials for technology, and some of the areas I have worked with are: Augmented Reality, Data Mining, Smartwatches, Shape-Changing Interfaces, sensors, actuators, and many more.

Personally, I enjoy spending time with my girlfriend and friends where we often play board games (also advanced ones), and enjoy a nice beer or two. Additionally, I love sports, especially badminton, which I have played since I was very young. I am very active on and off the court, as I am a board member for my club. My responsibilities are: creating events, maintaining it-systems, and make decisions to secure the future of the club.
